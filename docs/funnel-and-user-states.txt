1. Guest
   Auth/Plan: Guest
   Product Progress: No progress
   Feature Usage: None
   Financial State: No payment
   Behavioral State: Anonymous
   Core Event: page_view / cta_click_build_resume
   KPI: CTR به Signup
   Why it matters: کاربری که هنوز هیچ تعهدی نداده. از تبلیغ، سرچ ارگانیک، بلاگ یا معرفی وارد سایت شده و فقط در حال مشاهده است. Guest بد یعنی هزار تا Signup بی‌کیفیت، Guest خوب یعنی Activation بالا حتی با ترافیک کمتر.
   Team focus: پیام واضح، CTA شفاف، حذف حواس‌پرتی. اینجا هنوز فروش مهم نیست، درست دعوت کردن مهمه.
   Email/Notification Strategy:
     Goal: Signup
     Channel: On-site message (Email نداریم)
     Tone: Hero copy شفاف، ارزش فوری
     Example: Build a Dubai-ready CV in minutes. No signup required to start.
     Not allowed: Popup زودهنگام، درخواست ایمیل قبل از دیدن ارزش

   Team focus: ساده‌سازی Verification، توضیح شفاف «چرا باید تأیید کنی». اینجا UX و اعتماد تعیین‌کننده‌اند.

     Tone: آرام، توضیحی، غیرتهدیدی

2) ALERT RULES (State-based) — from Excel
========================
1. Activation Drop Alert
   From → To: 3 (Registered – No Resume) → 14 (Dormant)
   Trigger: no resume_started AND no_event ≥ 7 days
   Action: Email nudging + simplify first step

2. Resume Abandonment Alert
   From → To: 4 (Started Resume – Incomplete) → 14 (Dormant)
   Trigger: resume_started AND resume_not_completed AND inactivity ≥ 3 days
   Action: In-app reminder + Email: “Resume saved, You’re just a few steps away”
   Deadline: 24h
   KPI: Resume completion rate
   PostHog/GA4/Mixpanel: resume_started AND NOT resume_completed within 3 days

3. Free User Conversion Opportunity
   From → To: 7 (Free – Feature Blocked) → 14 (Dormant)
   Trigger: locked_feature_clicked AND no_upgrade AND inactivity ≥ 2 days
   Action: Email with exact feature benefit & calculator + contextual upgrade modal
   Deadline: 48h
   KPI: Free → Paid conversion

4. Buyer’s Remorse Alert
   From → To: 8 (Paid – Just Converted) → 14 (Dormant)
   Trigger: payment_success AND no_paid_feature_used ≥ 48 hours
   Action: Guided onboarding + Auto-open first paid feature + Tooltip walkthrough
   Deadline: 24–48h
   KPI: First paid action

5. Paid User Decay Alert
   From → To: 9 (Paid – Active) → 14 (Dormant)
   Trigger: paid_user AND no_event ≥ 7 days
   Action: Re-engagement email + value reminder (“You still have X credits”)
   Deadline: 7 days

6. Power User Burnout Alert
   From → To: 10 (Paid – Power User) → 14 (Dormant)
   Trigger: high_usage_last_14_days AND sudden_drop ≥ 80%
   Action: Personal email / credit gift + “What are you trying to achieve now?”
   Deadline: 48h

7. Upsell Timing Alert
   From → To: 11 (Credit Exhausted) → 14 (Dormant)
   Trigger: credit_exhausted AND no_topup ≥ 48 hours
   Action: Credit top-up offer (light, not desperate)

8. Payment Failure Risk
   From → To: 13 (Payment Failed) → 14 (Dormant)
   Trigger: payment_failed AND no_retry ≥ 24 hours
   Action: Retry reminder + support link + clear error explanation
   Deadline: 24h

9. Churn Prediction Alert (پیش‌دستانه)
   From → To: Any Active State (5–10) → 15 (Churn-risk)
   Trigger: usage_decline ≥ 60% over 7 days
   Action: Preventive re-engagement before dormancy
   PostHog: event_count_last_7_days <= 40% of previous 7 days

10–16: در فایل خالی است و نیاز به تعریف دارد.
